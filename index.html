U.S. Renewable Energy Power Plants

Comparing Biomass, Geothermal, and Solar Generating Capacity

Map authored by Nitin Yagnala

This map displays renewable energy power plants across the United States. Circle size represents generating capacity in megawatts (MW). Data sourced from U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA).

I made visual improvement by adding a legend which explained the meaning of circle sizes. I made interactive improvements by having the page zoom to the plant when clicked on. I also added a search by state filter. Lastly, I added the option to toggle on and off the dark base map from the open street base map. These changes make the map easier to navigate and understand the meaning of.
